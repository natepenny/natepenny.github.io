As part of a multidisciplinary team at the University of Tulsa, I contributed to the electrical integration of an accessible RC bulldozer controller. 

**My Contributions:**
* **Circuit Assembly:** Performed precision soldering for the button matrix and internal wiring.
* **Component Housing:** Assisted in the assembly of the 3D-printed ergonomic chassis designed by the team.
* **Testing:** Validated signal consistency between the custom controller and the RC receiver.

**Team Achievement:** The final prototype successfully allowed users with restricted fine motor skills to operate the vehicle using large-format inputs.
