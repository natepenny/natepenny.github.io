As the Lead Electrical Engineer for the Boomer Rocket Team (BRT) at OU, I am responsible for the design and integration of all avionics hardware and payload systems for our 2026 NASA Student Launch competition entry.

**Key Technical Contributions:**
* **Schematic Design:** Created and maintained all official avionics schematics using SkyCAD and Lucidchart.
* **Documentation Support:** Provided all electrical diagrams and system architecture visuals for the Preliminary (PDR) and Critical Design Reviews (CDR).
* **System Integration:** Engineered the electrical interface between flight computers, GPS telemetry, and the payload deployment sub-systems.
* **Hardware Validation:** Led the bench testing and continuity verification for all avionics sleds.
